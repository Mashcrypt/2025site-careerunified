We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling information responsibly in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa and generally accepted data-protection principles.

By using Career Unified, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

3. Information We Collect

Career Unified collects only limited information necessary to operate and improve the platform:

  • Search terms and filters used on the site

We do not require account registration and do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as ID numbers, banking details, or passwords.

4. CV Generator & Local Device Storage

The CV Generator operates entirely within your browser using local storage. This means:

  • You are solely responsible for managing and protecting this data

Clearing your browser storage will permanently remove saved CV information.

Under POPIA, you have the right to:
